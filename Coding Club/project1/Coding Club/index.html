Featuring

Our Coding club frequently engages in hands-on projects or coding challenges. These projects could range from creating simple applications to developing complex software systems. Working on projects allows members to apply their skills in a practical manner and gain experience in software development.

To enhance learning, We invite guest speakers from the industry or academia. These speakers might share insights on emerging technologies, career paths in tech, or provide workshops on specialized topics.

Beyond coding skills, We also focus on improving related skills such as problem-solving, algorithm design, debugging, and project management. These skills are crucial for becoming proficient software developers.
